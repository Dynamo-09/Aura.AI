# Aura Voice and Aura Intelligence Commands & Questions

Here is a comprehensive list of all the commands and questions you can ask Aura Voice and Aura Intelligence.

## 🎙️ Aura Voice Commands (Voice Assistant)

### Wake Words
* "Hi Aura"
* "Hey Aura"
* "Hello Aura"
* "Hai Aura"

### Navigation Commands
* "Take me to the dashboard" (or "command center")
* "Go to market pulse" (or "analysis")
* "Open the AI scanner"
* "Navigate to neural intelligence"
* "Take me to Aura assistant"
* "Open my portfolio"
* "Go to settings"

### Stock Search
* "Show me the stock of [Stock Name/Ticker]" (e.g., "Show me the stock of Apple")
* "Search for [Stock Name/Ticker]" (e.g., "Search for Tesla")
* "Show me [Stock Name/Ticker] stock"

### Currency Settings
* "Change currency to USD" (or "Dollar")
* "Change currency to INR" (or "Rupee")
* "Change currency to Euro"
* "Change currency to Pound"
* "Change currency to Australian Dollar"
* "Change currency to Hong Kong Dollar"

### Account Management
* "Delete my account" (Followed by "Yes" or "No" when prompted)
* "Logout" (Followed by "Yes" or "No" when prompted)
